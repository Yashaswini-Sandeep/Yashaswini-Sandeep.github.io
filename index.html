Seasonal Demand Forecasting with ARIMA

Analyzed 48+ months of balcony-railing search data, uncovering a repeatable March–May demand peak with average monthly volume of ~3.2K and highs of ~6.7K.

Applied end-to-end time series diagnostics in R, validating non-stationarity (ADF p > 0.28) and isolating strong seasonality to improve forecast reliability.

Built and compared multiple forecasting models, selecting Auto ARIMA(1,1,0)(0,1,0)[12] as the best fit for a 12-month forecast horizon with AIC 356.99, RMSE 842.92, and MAPE 10.49%.

Translated forecast results into business actions by showing that seasonality not "long-term" trend drove demand, with month effects statistically significant (p = 0.006) despite a modest R² = 0.22, supporting earlier campaign and inventory planning.
